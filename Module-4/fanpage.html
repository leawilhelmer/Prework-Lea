Bio

Luke Combs is an American country music singer and songwriter. He was born on March 2, 1990 in Charlotte, North Carolina and he later moved to Nashville, Tennesse to pursue a career in music. He got married to Nicole Hocking in August 2020. He has released two Albums, "This One's for You" and "What You See Is What You Get".
